Your Personalized Skincare Recommendations

Dry Skin:

Goal: Lock in moisture, soothe, and protect dry skin.

Products:

Cleanser: Gentle, non-foaming cleanser like Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser or Neutrogena Hydrating Facial Cleanser. Toner: Hydrating toner like Thayers Rose Petal Witch Hazel or Pixi Glow Tonic. Moisturizer: Rich, hydrating moisturizer like CeraVe Moisturizing Cream or Laneige Water Bank Moisturizing Cream. Eye Cream: Hydrating eye cream like Kiehl's Avocado Eye Cream or La Roche-Posay Pigmentclar Eye Cream. Sunscreen: Broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30, like EltaMD UV Clear Broad-Spectrum SPF 46 or La Roche-Posay Anthelios Melt-In Sunscreen Milk.

Routine:

1.Cleanse with a gentle cleanser, massaging it onto the skin for 30 seconds.

Toner: Sweep a cotton pad soaked in toner across the face, avoiding the eye area.

Eye Cream: Pat a small amount of eye cream under the peepers.

Sunscreen: Apply sunscreen liberally, 15-30 minutes before going outside.
