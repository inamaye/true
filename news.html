Latest News in Skincare

There have been several recent developments in the field of skincare. Here are some of the latest news:

Researchers have made a breakthrough in developing a prosthetic hand that can feel touch like a human hand, which could also be used to help restore lost functionality to patients after a skin graft.

A new study has found that melanoma, an aggressive form of skin cancer, is often detected later in people with darker skin complexions, leading to poorer outcomes.

Scientists have developed a potential new treatment for alopecia areata, an autoimmune disorder that causes hair loss, using a microneedle patch that delivers immune-regulating molecules.
